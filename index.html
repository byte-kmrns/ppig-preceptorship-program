How the program works

1

Explore & Connect

Students express interest and are matched with a private practice preceptor.

2

Shadow & Learn

Complete up to 16 hours of shadowing and gain insight into clinical care and practice operations.

3

Reflect & Complete

Students complete program requirements and submit verification to fulfill shadowing hours.

For Students

  • Fulfill your 16 required shadowing hours
  • Explore private practice patient care
  • Learn about practice operations and workflows
  • Build relationships and professional networks
  • Clarify career interests early

For Preceptors

  • Mentor future clinicians
  • Share the value of private practice medicine
  • Host flexible shadowing experiences
  • Strengthen the DFW medical community
  • Inspire the next generation

16-hour commitment

This program is designed for first-year medical students to complete 16 required shadowing hours in a private practice setting.

Eligible specialties & providers

All clinical specialties are welcome. PPIG is seeking MD, DO, PA, and NP preceptors practicing in the DFW area in a private practice setting.
